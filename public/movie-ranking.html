THE Best Movies According To Haji

My top 3 indian movies of all time

sooryavansham

Heera fails to live up to expectations of his father Thakur Bhanupratap Singh,
a powerful and respected man of his community.
Despite all odds Heera struggles to keep his father's pride.

golam

A mysterious death during work hours in an office puzzles all but only Investigator
ASP Sandeep Krishna observes it as a murder.

singham

A truly honest police officer is transferred to a town controlled by a gangster he has humiliated.
The gangster believes he can use good power to bring down this officer
who made him look foolish and weak
